What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information and to enable certain functionalities. Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser. They serve various purposes, from remembering your login details to analyzing site traffic. Essentially, cookies allow a website to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another. This enhances your browsing experience significantly, making it smoother and more tailored to your habits. Without cookies, many modern website features would simply not be possible or would be far less convenient. They are a fundamental technology in today's internet landscape, facilitating everything from shopping carts to personalized content recommendations. Understanding their role is crucial for managing your online privacy effectively. Our commitment is to use cookies responsibly, always with your experience and data security in mind.
